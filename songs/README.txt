HOW TO ADD YOUR SONGS
====================

The playlist is now set up with HTML5 audio players that play directly on your website.

STEPS TO ADD YOUR SONGS:
1. Place your MP3 music files in this folder (songs/)
2. Name them as: song1.mp3, song2.mp3, song3.mp3, ... song20.mp3
3. The files will automatically play in the playlist

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- MP3 (.mp3)
- WAV (.wav)
- OGG (.ogg)
- AAC (.aac)

EXAMPLE:
- song1.mp3 → A Thousand Years
- song2.mp3 → All of Me
- song3.mp3 → Perfect
... and so on up to song20.mp3

All 20 songs are already configured in the HTML with their titles and descriptions.
Just add your audio files here and they will work immediately!

FILE STRUCTURE:
vltinday/
├── songs/
│   ├── song1.mp3
│   ├── song2.mp3
│   ├── song3.mp3
│   └── ... (up to song20.mp3)
├── playlist.html
├── style.css
└── other files...
